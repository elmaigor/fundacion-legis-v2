Cookies Policy

This website, owned by Fundación Legis, uses cookies to improve user experience, analyze browsing habits, and ensure proper website functionality.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, login details, and overall user activity to provide a more personalized experience.

2. Types of cookies used

  • Technical cookies: Necessary for website operation and navigation.
  • Preference cookies: Allow the website to remember your settings such as language or region.
  • Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with the site, anonymously, to improve content and usability.

3. Third-party cookies

We may use analytical services such as Google Analytics to collect statistical and anonymous information about usage patterns. These cookies are managed by third parties outside Fundación Legis. You can learn more at: Google Cookies Policy.

4. Cookie management and settings

You can manage, block, or delete cookies at any time from your browser settings. Each browser provides different options for privacy and cookie management:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Internet Explorer / Edge

5. Consent

By continuing to browse our website after being informed of our cookies policy, you consent to the use of cookies as described above. You may withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your browser settings.

6. Updates to this policy

Fundación Legis reserves the right to modify this Cookies Policy to adapt it to legal or technical updates. Users are encouraged to review this document periodically.
