What We Do

At Fundación Legis we sow hope to harvest the future through two different projects united by one essential value: the dignity of the human being.

Prison Assistance Project (PAP)

We provide pro bono legal aid and human support to people deprived of freedom, helping them rebuild their lives and defending their fundamental rights.

Learn more

Child Assistance Project (PAM)

We support pregnant mothers and newborn babies in situations of extreme poverty. Our goal is to offer protection, care and hope for life itself.
