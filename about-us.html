Who We Are

Fundación Legis was created to sow hope in the lives of people facing difficult situations. Born in Asturias, with a universal calling, our mission is to extend a helping hand and build a future of dignity for especially vulnerable groups — individuals deprived of freedom and minors at extreme risk.

With a holistic vision of the human being, embracing physical, emotional and spiritual dimensions, Fundación Legis, through its trustees and collaborators, is committed to developing projects that contribute to a fairer and more humane society.
