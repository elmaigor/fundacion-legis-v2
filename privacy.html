Privacy Policy

In accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation - GDPR) and the Spanish Organic Law 3/2018 (LOPDGDD), Fundación Legis informs users of its privacy policy regarding the processing and protection of personal data.

1. Data Controller

Fundación Legis
Registered in Asturias, Spain.
Email: info@fundacionlegis.org
Website: https://fundacionlegis.org

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The personal data collected through the contact forms, donation forms, or email correspondence are processed for the following purposes:

  • To respond to user requests and inquiries.
  • To manage collaboration or volunteering offers.
  • To process and confirm donations or contributions.
  • To send institutional or project-related communications, provided prior consent is granted.

3. Legal Basis

The processing of your data is based on your consent when voluntarily providing your information, and, where applicable, on the legitimate interest of Fundación Legis in maintaining contact with its collaborators.

4. Data Retention

Data will be retained for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which they were collected, or until you request their deletion.

5. Data Sharing

Fundación Legis does not transfer or share personal data with third parties, except when required by law or when necessary for the proper management of donations or institutional activities, always ensuring compliance with data protection regulations.

6. User Rights

You may exercise your rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability and objection by sending an email to info@fundacionlegis.org, indicating the right you wish to exercise and providing proof of identity.

7. Security Measures

Fundación Legis implements appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure data security, confidentiality and integrity, preventing unauthorized access, alteration or loss of personal information.

8. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated to comply with legal changes or adapt to new circumstances. Users are advised to review this page periodically.
