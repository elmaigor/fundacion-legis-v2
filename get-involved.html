Tax Benefits

When you donate to Fundación Legis, your contribution entitles you to tax deductions under Spanish law. The first €250 donated per year can be deducted at 80% in your personal income tax return. ... Beyond this amount, the deduction rate is 40% (or 45% if you have been donating to the same foundation for at least three consecutive years). Legal entities (companies) can deduct 35% of the donation, or 40% if they maintain recurring support.

These benefits are established by the Spanish Law 49/2002 on the tax regime for non-profit entities and tax incentives for patronage. We will send you a donation certificate at the end of the fiscal year so you can include it in your tax return.
