Transparency

Fundación Legis is committed to active transparency. We publish relevant information about our governance, projects and finances in a clear and accessible manner.

Institutional Information

  • Mission and Purpose: To sow hope and promote assistance projects for people deprived of freedom and for children at risk.
  • Governing Bodies / Board of Trustees: (Composition and profiles will be published here.)
  • Statutes: A PDF download will be made available once approved.
  • Contact: info@fundacionlegis.org

Financial Information

  • Annual Accounts and Activity Report: Will be published after formal approval and, where required, audit.
  • Funding Policy: Funds are allocated to the PAP (Prison Assistance Project) and PAM (Child Assistance Project), following criteria of efficiency, accountability and impact.
  • Donations and Certificates: Donors receive a certificate for tax purposes pursuant to Spanish Law 49/2002 on the tax regime for non-profit entities.

Public Funding and Grants

If Fundación Legis receives public subsidies or aid, the details (call, amount, granting body and purpose) will be published here, together with the corresponding justification documents where applicable.

Integrity and Compliance

We apply criteria of legality, honesty and good governance in all our activities and relationships with beneficiaries, partners and suppliers. We promote data protection, child safeguarding and ethical conduct across our projects and collaborations.
